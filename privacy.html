Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data with client-side processing.

Introduction

Welcome to FizTools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure experience.

Key Principle: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most online services, FizTools is designed with privacy as the core feature. We do NOT collect:

  • Your Files: All PDF and image processing happens in your browser
  • Personal Information: No registration or login required
  • Usage Data: We don't track which tools you use or how often
  • IP Addresses: We don't log or store visitor IP addresses
  • Cookies: No tracking cookies or analytics cookies
  • Location Data: We don't collect geographic information

How Client-Side Processing Works

FizTools uses a revolutionary approach to online file processing:

  1. File Selection: You select files from your device
  2. Local Processing: Files are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript
  3. No Upload: Files never leave your computer
  4. Download Result: Processed files are downloaded directly to your device
  5. Automatic Cleanup: Temporary data is cleared when you close the tab

This architecture ensures maximum privacy and security for your sensitive documents.

Browser Storage

FizTools may use minimal browser storage for:

  • Local Storage: To remember your tool preferences (like page size, orientation)
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage during file processing (cleared when you close the browser)
  • Cache: Browser cache for faster loading of JavaScript libraries

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings, and it will not affect the functionality of our tools.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect limited information:

Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use CDNs to serve JavaScript libraries (PDF.js, jsPDF, etc.). These services may collect anonymous usage statistics for their own purposes.

Font Services

Google Fonts may collect anonymized usage data. You can read Google's privacy policy for more information.

No Advertising Networks

We do not currently use advertising networks, but if we add non-intrusive ads in the future (to support development), we will update this policy accordingly.

Security Measures

While our client-side processing architecture inherently provides security by keeping files on your device, we also implement:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections to our website are encrypted
  • Secure Code Practices: Regular security reviews of our code
  • No Backend Storage: No servers to hack or compromise
  • Browser Security: Leveraging built-in browser security features

Children's Privacy

FizTools is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, this is inherently protected.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page
  • Adding a notification banner on our website for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Your Rights

Under various privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we don't collect personal data, these rights are inherently respected:

  • Right to Access: You can see what (little) data we might have
  • Right to Deletion: Clear your browser storage to delete preferences
  • Right to Opt-Out: No data collection to opt-out from
  • Right to Portability: No personal data to transfer

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Contact Page.

We're committed to transparency and welcome any questions about how we protect your privacy.
