About FizTools

Learn about our mission to provide free, privacy-focused PDF tools for everyone

Our Mission

At FizTools, we believe that everyone should have access to professional-grade PDF tools without compromising their privacy or paying expensive fees. Our mission is to provide a suite of powerful PDF tools that work entirely in your browser, ensuring your documents never leave your device.

Founded on the principles of privacy, accessibility, and user empowerment, we've created a platform that puts you in complete control of your PDF processing needs.

Why Choose Client-Side Processing?

Unlike traditional online PDF tools that require uploading your sensitive documents to remote servers, FizTools processes everything locally in your browser. This approach offers several key advantages:

  • Maximum Privacy: Your files never leave your computer
  • Enhanced Security: No risk of data breaches or unauthorized access
  • Faster Processing: No waiting for file uploads or server processing
  • Offline Capability: Works without internet connection after page load
  • No File Limits: Process files of any size without restrictions

Our Features

Image to PDF Converter

Convert multiple images (JPG, PNG, WEBP) into a single, high-quality PDF document. Customize page size, orientation, margins, and image quality to create professional PDFs from your photos and images.

PDF Merger

Combine multiple PDF files into one unified document while preserving the quality of each page. Reorder pages, maintain formatting, and create comprehensive documents from separate files.

All in One Page PDF

Create stunning PDF collages by arranging all pages of a PDF document on a single page. Choose between grid, horizontal, or vertical layouts, and customize thumbnail sizes for the perfect overview document.

Technical Excellence

FizTools is built using modern web technologies including:

  • PDF.js: For PDF rendering and processing
  • PDF-Lib: For advanced PDF manipulation
  • jsPDF: For PDF creation and generation
  • HTML5 Canvas: For image processing and rendering
  • Vanilla JavaScript: For optimal performance and compatibility

Our tools work across all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and are fully responsive, providing an excellent experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
